Shopping For Books

This website sells books with a few genres which are: Comics, Food, Horror, Mystery and Sport. Books can be found by using the search bar, simply put the same of the book into the search box and press the search icon. Other ways books can be found are by using the price, genre and published date dropdowns. Add any book to the cart and simply choose the number of books you would like to purchase. In the cart, the items which you have selected will show with the total amount. Change the quantity of a book easily by entering in a new quantity number followed by the blue edit icon button.

The Forum

In the forums page, customers are able to share their ideas and start posts relating to the books on the website. Users can reply inside posts if they would like to give feedback to a certain post. The forum posts are ordered by the date they were posted (newest first).

To view the forum page, an account must be created!
